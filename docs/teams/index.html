    Datathon Teams

    You can compete in teams of between 2 - 5 current NYU students. Teams can consist of any number of undergraduate students and maximum 2 graduate students. We highly encourage that at least one team member comes from a field outside data science or computer science. Keep checking this page for the team entry link!
